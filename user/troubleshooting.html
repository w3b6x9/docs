Troubleshooting¶

Known issues¶

  • Boot stalls during Replicate system image to swap-based memdisk: This build of the ISO is damaged. Please wait for another continuous build to appear and try again.

  • Boots to a non-graphical login: prompt: Your grahpics hardware was not detected properly. Please try on another machine.

Getting help¶

Developers chat at #FuryBSD on irc.freenode.net. Since we are working in different timezones, do not expect immediate answers. It is best to stay connected for at least a couple of days or so.

Reporting bugs¶

Please search https://github.com/helloSystem/ISO/issues for known issues. Feel free to add to existing issues or to open new issues there.
